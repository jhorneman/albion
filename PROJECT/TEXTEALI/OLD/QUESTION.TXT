	*** Designing a computer game ***

- DOES A GAME-DESIGNER NEED TO BE A PROGRAMMER ?

- WHAT KIND OF GAMES ARE THERE ?
Alright, that's a mean one, I admit. I will just give my list, let me know if
I missed any or if you feel other subdivisions are possible.

- IN WHAT FORMAT DOES ONE PUT A GAME DESIGN ?
Or in other words, is there a way to describe any computer game ? Is there a
list of items which one should simply check off ? Does it depend on the type
of game ?

- WHAT MAKES PEOPLE WANT TO KEEP PLAYING A GAME ?
What elements keep players motivated ?

- WHAT ELEMENTS MAKE A GAME GOOD ?
This question concerns games in general as well as specific game types.


	*** Producing a computer game ***

- WHAT ABOUT EDITORS ?
When should one use them ? What should they do ? How much effort should one
put in them ? How can games be improved by the use of editors ? How does
using an editor affect production ?

- HOW CAN ONE PRODUCE A GAME ?
Sorry, I saw no other way to phrase this question. What I mean is, there are
several possibilities for a game developer to work :
	- Free-lance
	- Development team
	- Big software company
	and maybe more ?
What are the advantages and disadvantages of each ?

- HOW MANY PEOPLE DOES ONE NEED TO PRODUCE A GAME ?
Can certain games only be developed by large groups ? How many programmers,
graphics artists does one need ?
